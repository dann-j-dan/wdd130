History

It all started with a love for adventures and the roar of the river. Back in 1998, a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts decided to turn their weekend hobby into something more. Fueled by a deep respect for nature and a desire to share the thrill of white water rafting with others, they launched White Water Rafting Company with just a couple of rafts, a pickup truck, and big dreams. From those humble beginnings on the banks of the Smokey Nile, the company quickly earned a reputation for safety, fun, and unforgettable experiences.

What began as a small, family-run business has grown into one of the region’s most trusted rafting outfitters, known for top-notch equipment, certified guides, and personalized trips for adventurers of all ages. Over the years, we’ve taken thousands of guests down scenic rivers, through wild rapids, and past breathtaking landscapes. Whether it’s a first-time rafter or a seasoned thrill-seeker, every guest becomes part of the White-Water Rafting Company family and we enjoy the rafts together in harmony.
