Overview

Purpose

Our purpose of the white water rafting site is to help people get down the river, teach them to paddle both adult and youth and also tell them about the river ecology, and handle every detail of the trip. Guides are available to take care of every individual need on the trip. And also our white water company also provides help to customers to navigate rough waters by supplying them with the equipment and guides they need to safety head down the river.

Audience

In case you weren't aware, whitewater rafting can be one of the most adrenaline-pumping, thrill-seeking activities out there. From watching the horizon line disappear out of sight, signaling that you're about to drop 15 feet in 6 seconds, to paddling for your life through Class IV+ rapids, it's a wild ride.Size matters when it comes to paddling and staying in the raft. Generally speaking, kids under 12 are best suited for trips up to Class III. Twelve year olds and a little older up may be able to handle a Class IV challenge depending on the amount of paddling help that the guide may need in the rapids.
